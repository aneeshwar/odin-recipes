Lazy Ground Turkey Lasagna

Description

No fuss, quick and easy clean up lasagna dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of ground turkey
  • 1 finely diced onion
  • 28 ounces of jarred pasta sauce
  • 3 pounds of frozen cheese ravioli
  • 4 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat over to 375 degrees Farenheit.
  2. In a pan over medium heat, brown the ground turkey and the onions. Drain excess fat, add the spaghetti sauce and let simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. In a baking dish, create a bottom layer with the spaghetti sauce and ground turkey. Follow this with a layer of ravioli and continue to do so alternating between sauce, ravioli and cheese.
  4. Bake uncovered in a preheated oven for 1 hour. Let sit and serve!
