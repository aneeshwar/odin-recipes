Gochujang Fried Chicken

Description

Gochujang Fried Chicken is a Korean style fried chicken that features a blend of sweet and spicy flavors.

Ingredients

  • 1/3rd cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 lb of chicken tenderloins
  • 1/4 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of potato starch
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • vegetable oil for frying
  • 1/4 cup of honey
  • 2 tablesppons of soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang paste
  • 2 teaspoons of red pepper flakes

Directions

  1. Whisk the milk and egg together in a large bowl until well combined, add chicken tenders to egg wash and marinate in the fridge for 15 minutes.
  2. Combine flour, potato starch, salt and pepper in a large mixing bowl. Pour mixture into a large, resealable plastic bag. Drain chicken from marinade, add it to the bag, seal it and shake until the chicken is coated well.
  3. Heat oil in a large saucepan to 325 degrees Farenheit.
  4. Fry chicken in batches in the preheated oil for 2 minutes, transfer to a paper towel lined rack to drain.
  5. Increase the oil heat to 375 Farenheit and refry the chicken for 2 minutes.
  6. Heat a small saucepan over medium heat, add honey, brown sugar, soy sauce, gochujang paste and red pepper flakes. Cook until sugar has melted and drizzle over chicken.
